Player

The player allows to play any kind of musical object. Usual Start, Play, Stop and Loop commands are available. A menu allows to choose between different mode of synchronization:

  • Internal synchronization: the musical object will be played with a default tempo of 120 bpm.
  • Clock synchronization: the player can be synchronized with an external source and will respond to start, stop, continue, and clock MIDI events.

When an abstraction is placed in the player, only its "body" will be played.
